## EZY Support

Welcome to EZY Support. We’re here to help you have a smooth and safe experience using EZY apps.

---

### 📱 About EZY

EZY is a mobility and safety platform designed for:

* Parents & families
* Schools & institutions
* Corporate transport teams
* Fleet & logistics operators
* Drivers & transport partners

It enables real-time trip tracking, safety alerts, and seamless communication between users and drivers.

---

### 🚗 Driver & Partner Support

If you are using **EZY Partner (Driver App)** and need help with:

* Starting or ending trips
* Live location sharing issues
* Trip history or records
* Profile verification (KYC)
* App performance or login issues

Please contact our support team.

---

### 👨‍👩‍👧 User App Support

If you are using the **EZY User App** and need help with:

* Live trip tracking
* Safety alerts or notifications
* SOS functionality
* Driver verification details
* Account or access issues

We’re here to assist you.

---

### 🛠️ Technical Support

Facing technical issues like:

* App not loading properly
* Tracking not updating
* Notifications not working

Please share the following details for faster resolution:

* Device type (Android / iOS)
* App version
* Description of the issue
* Screenshots (if possible)

### 🔒 Privacy & Safety

EZY follows a privacy-first approach:

* Location data is used only during active trips
* Driver data is collected only for verification
* Your data is secure and never used for advertising
